# Sample Post

This is a sample blog post to demonstrate how RSS feeds work in Quartz 4.

## What you'll see

When you have content like this, your RSS feed will include:
- The title of the post
- A link to the full post
- The description
- The publication date

## How to use RSS

RSS feeds are meant to be consumed by RSS readers, not viewed directly in browsers. You can:

1. **Subscribe with an RSS reader** like Feedly, Inoreader, or NetNewsWire
2. **Add the feed URL** to your reader: `https://neema.github.io/snr/index.xml`
3. **Get notified** when you publish new content

## Why the XML looks "broken"

When you view an RSS feed directly in a browser, it shows as raw XML because browsers don't have built-in RSS styling. This is normal! RSS feeds are designed for machines to read, not humans to view directly.
